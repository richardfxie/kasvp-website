Julian Handel

Partner

Julian is a Partner at KAS Venture Partners. Before joining the firm, he was a Managing Director at Anchorage Capital Group, where he spent 9 years working across a variety of debt and equity investments, and also previously worked at 40 North Industries and Silver Lake Partners. Julian graduated with a dual degree from the Huntsman Program for International Studies and Business at the University of Pennsylvania in 2011.
